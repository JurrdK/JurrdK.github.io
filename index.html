About Me

My Story

Growing up, I was the kid who didn't just play games — I took them apart. I'd study how levels were built, experiment with game mechanics, and dream up worlds long before I knew how to code them.

That curiosity led me to Roblox, where I taught myself game development from the ground up. What started as experiments quickly turned into real games — and Island Royale, a battle royale I built and maintained as the sole developer, became a breakout hit with over 432 million visits.

I've since built a catalog of experiences spanning combat arenas, survival games, and more — always focused on tight gameplay, player engagement, and pushing what's possible on the platform.

When I'm not building games, you'll find me exploring new engines, breaking down what makes great games tick, or figuring out new ways to keep players hooked.

First Games

Gladiators & Slithery Snakes

Jumped into Roblox and shipped my first real games. Gladiators brought arena combat to the platform, and Slithery Snakes added a fun competitive twist. These early projects proved I could build and ship complete experiences.

Leveling Up

Developed Slaughtering Floor and deepened skills in Lua scripting, multiplayer networking, and player-centric design. Games started gaining real traction.
